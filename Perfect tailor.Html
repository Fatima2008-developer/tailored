About Us

Hausa traditional clothing is known for it's flowing forms,vibrant colors,and intricate embroidery.From atamfa to zani,dogon riga,jallabiya and gele,every peice tells a story of heritage and class.
In Nothern Nigeria,dressing is more than just fashion_it's identity,just as the saying goes from the hausas that "kaya shine mutum"(clothes make a man).
